- Km 8.5-10: able to increase pace again without calf worsening — actually felt slightly better. Last 500m hard on effort but calf was not the limiter.
- Key difference from Mar 4: ran through the calf discomfort instead of stopping. It did not worsen beyond 4/10 and partially resolved in the final km. Longest run since Feb 18 onset.
